Bullish Strategies

Long Call
BULLISH · UNLIMITED UPSIDE

Bull Call Spread
MILDLY BULLISH · CAPPED
Buy lower K call, sell higher K call. Reduce cost, cap profit. Best for moderate upside.
Max Profit: Δstrike − net prem  |  Max Loss: Net prem
💵
Covered Call
NEUTRAL-BULLISH · INCOME
Hold stock + sell call. Premium income. Caps upside at strike. Good for sideways markets.
Max Profit: Premium + (K−S₀)  |  Capped upside
Bearish Strategies
📉
Long Put
BEARISH · DOWNSIDE PROTECTION
Buy a put. Profit if stock falls below strike − premium. Also used as portfolio insurance.
Max Profit: K − Premium  |  Max Loss: Premium
🐻
Bear Put Spread
MILDLY BEARISH · CAPPED
Buy higher K put, sell lower K put. Reduce cost, cap protection. Best for moderate fall.
Max Profit: Δstrike − net prem  |  Max Loss: Net prem
☂️
Protective Put
BEARISH · PORTFOLIO INSURANCE
Hold stock + buy put. Floors your loss. Upside fully preserved.
Max Loss: S₀ − K + Premium  |  Upside unlimited
Volatility / Neutral Strategies
⚡
Long Straddle
HIGH VOLATILITY EXPECTED
Buy ATM call + ATM put (same K). Profit from big move either way. Best before events.
Profit if |move| > total premium paid
🦉
Long Strangle
HIGH VOLATILITY · CHEAPER
Buy OTM call + OTM put (different Ks). Cheaper than straddle, needs bigger move.
Profit if price goes beyond either B/E
🦃
Butterfly Spread
LOW VOLATILITY · NEUTRAL
Buy 1 low + sell 2 ATM + buy 1 high call. Profit if price stays near middle K.
Max profit at middle strike  |  Limited risk
